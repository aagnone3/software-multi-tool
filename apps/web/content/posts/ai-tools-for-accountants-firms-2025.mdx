Accounting is one of the professions most affected by AI automation — and also one of the biggest beneficiaries. The routine, high-volume document work that used to take hours can now be done in minutes.

## What AI Can Handle for Accountants

### Invoice Processing at Scale
Processing vendor invoices manually is expensive. Staff manually key in vendor names, amounts, line items, and GL codes — and make mistakes. AI invoice processing extracts this data automatically with accuracy exceeding 95% for standard invoice formats.

For a firm processing 500 invoices per month, AI can save 40+ hours of data entry work.

### Expense Categorization
Client expense reports come in all shapes and sizes. AI can categorize expenses by type, flag unusual patterns, and match transactions to budget categories automatically.

**Accuracy benchmark:** Well-trained AI models achieve 90%+ accuracy on expense categorization for standard business expense types.

### Document Review and Summarization
Annual reports, audit documents, contracts with vendors — summarizing and extracting key financial data from long documents is now a minutes-long task instead of hours.

### Meeting Notes and Client Calls
Tax planning meetings, audit kick-offs, client review calls — AI transcription and summarization creates accurate records without anyone manually taking notes.

## The Competitive Advantage

Firms that have adopted AI document processing report:
- 50–70% reduction in time spent on invoice processing
- 30–40% reduction in expense report review time
- Higher client satisfaction (faster turnaround)
- Fewer data entry errors

The firms that adopt these tools first will serve more clients with the same headcount — or reduce headcount while maintaining service levels.

## Where to Start

Invoice processing and expense categorization have the clearest ROI and the lowest adoption risk. Start there, measure the time savings, then expand to other document types.

The tools exist today. The only question is whether you implement them before your competitors do.
