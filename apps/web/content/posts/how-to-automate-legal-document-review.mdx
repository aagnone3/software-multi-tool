# How to Automate Legal Document Review with AI

Legal document review is one of the most time-consuming and expensive tasks in law. Whether you're reviewing contracts in an in-house legal department, conducting due diligence for an acquisition, or responding to a discovery request, the manual process of reading and analyzing documents is a bottleneck that costs real money.

AI is changing this — and you don't need an enterprise legal tech budget to benefit.

## What Legal Document Review Actually Involves

At its core, legal document review means reading documents and answering questions like:

- What are the key terms and conditions?
- What are the risks and liabilities?
- Does this document contain X clause?
- Are there any unusual or non-standard provisions?
- How does this compare to our standard form?

Lawyers and paralegals do this manually today. It's slow, it's expensive, and it's prone to human error when volume is high.

## Where AI Makes the Biggest Difference

### Contract Review and Abstraction

AI contract review tools can read a contract and produce a structured summary in minutes. Instead of a paralegal spending 2–3 hours abstracting a 40-page commercial lease, an AI tool can:

- Extract key dates (commencement, expiration, option deadlines)
- Identify unusual or non-standard clauses
- Flag risk areas (indemnification, liability caps, IP ownership)
- Summarize payment obligations and penalties

**Practical result:** What used to take a junior associate 3 hours takes 3 minutes. Associates focus on judgment and strategy; AI handles extraction.

### Due Diligence Document Review

M&A due diligence involves reviewing hundreds or thousands of contracts, employment agreements, IP assignments, and regulatory filings. Manual review is measured in attorney-hours; AI review is measured in minutes.

AI tools can:
- Classify documents by type
- Extract key fields across a document set
- Flag contracts with change-of-control provisions
- Identify missing or non-standard terms relative to a checklist

### Policy and Compliance Review

In-house legal teams regularly review vendor agreements, customer contracts, and internal policies for compliance with regulatory requirements or company standards.

AI document review tools can screen documents against a defined playbook — flagging clauses that deviate from acceptable parameters before the document reaches a lawyer for review.

## What AI Document Review Doesn't Replace

AI document review augments lawyers; it doesn't replace them. AI tools are excellent at:

- Extraction and classification
- Identifying what's present (or absent) in a document
- Pattern matching against known clause types
- Summarizing long documents

They are not substitutes for:

- Legal judgment and advice
- Negotiation strategy
- Novel legal issues with no precedent
- Documents requiring specialized regulatory expertise

The right model is AI handling the high-volume, repetitive extraction work so that lawyers spend their time on analysis and judgment.

## Practical Implementation for In-House Teams

You don't need a dedicated legal tech platform to start. Here's a practical approach:

**Step 1: Identify your highest-volume review task**
Is it vendor NDA review? Employment agreement abstraction? Customer contract compliance screening? Pick one.

**Step 2: Run a pilot**
Upload 10–20 representative documents and review the AI output. Calibrate your expectations around accuracy and completeness.

**Step 3: Build a review workflow**
AI output is a starting point. Build a lightweight process for human review of AI-flagged items — especially risk areas.

**Step 4: Expand**
Once the first workflow is running smoothly, expand to adjacent use cases.
