# AI Tools for Legal Operations Teams in 2025

Legal operations (legal ops) teams sit at the intersection of law and business efficiency. Their job is to make the legal function faster, cheaper, and more strategic. AI tools are becoming essential to that mission.

## Why AI Matters for Legal Ops

Legal teams are drowning in documents. The average corporate legal team reviews thousands of contracts per year — and that's before you add board materials, regulatory filings, and internal memos.

AI tools help legal ops teams:

- **Review and summarize contracts** at 10x the speed
- **Extract key clauses** without reading every page
- **Generate meeting minutes** from legal team calls
- **Track obligations** across a large contract portfolio

## Top AI Tools for Legal Operations

The most-used AI tool in corporate legal is the contract analyzer. Upload any agreement and extract:

- Parties, dates, and key terms

Regulatory filings, court documents, and industry reports can be summarized and analyzed to extract the key points for your legal team.

## Legal Ops AI Use Cases in Practice

**Contract intake:** Every new contract goes through the AI contract analyzer before attorney review. Attorneys get a pre-populated summary sheet, cutting initial review time by more than half.

**Vendor management:** Upload MSAs and SOWs to extract key commercial terms and flag non-standard provisions automatically.

**Board and governance materials:** AI summarizes board minutes and resolutions for quick reference.

**Deposition prep:** Upload deposition transcripts to the meeting summarizer for speaker-labeled summaries and key testimony extraction.
