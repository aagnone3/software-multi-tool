# How to Automate Meeting Follow-Ups with AI

The meeting ends. You have decisions to document, action items to assign, and a follow-up email to draft. For most professionals, that's another 20-40 minutes of work—per meeting.

AI changes that completely.

## The Problem with Manual Meeting Follow-Ups

Most follow-up processes are broken:

- **Notes are incomplete** because you can't write and participate simultaneously
- **Action items get lost** between the meeting and when they're finally communicated
- **Delays reduce impact**—the longer after a meeting before follow-up goes out, the less momentum you maintain
- **Inconsistency** means different team members do (or don't do) follow-ups differently

## What AI-Powered Meeting Follow-Ups Look Like

With the right AI tools, here's the new workflow:

1. **Record or transcribe** the meeting (many tools do this automatically)
2. **AI processes the transcript** and extracts:
   - Key decisions made
   - Action items with owners and due dates
   - Open questions and next steps
   - A concise summary for stakeholders who didn't attend
3. **Review and send** in under 2 minutes

What used to take 30 minutes now takes 2.
