# How to Use AI for Sales Contract Review

Every sales rep knows the frustration: deal is done, customer is excited, then everything stalls in contract review. Legal is backlogged, redlines take a week, and sometimes the deal goes cold.

AI contract review is helping sales teams break this bottleneck.

## Why Sales Contract Review Is Slow

Legal teams are conservative — rightly so. But many of the contracts they review are standard MSAs, order forms, and service agreements that follow predictable patterns. AI can handle the pattern recognition, freeing legal for the real edge cases and negotiations.

## What Sales Teams Can Do with AI Contract Analysis

### Pre-Screening Before Legal
Before sending to legal, use AI to:
- Flag non-standard clauses (unlimited liability, unusual payment terms, IP ownership issues)
- Identify missing standard provisions (limitation of liability, warranty disclaimers)
- Summarize key commercial terms for sales leadership

This way, legal only sees contracts that need real attention — or gets a clear issue list to work from.

### Redline Summary
When customers send redlined versions, use AI to:
- Summarize what changed vs. your standard template
- Flag which changes are material vs. cosmetic
- Help sales understand the business impact of proposed changes

### Deal Summary for Handoff
When closing deals, generate a contract summary capturing: license scope, term, pricing, renewal terms, key obligations, and special provisions. This speeds up handoff from sales to implementation/success.

## Sample Workflow

1. Customer sends back executed or redlined MSA
2. Sales rep uploads to AI contract analyzer
3. AI returns: summary, flagged issues, comparison vs. standard terms
4. Rep reviews and decides: accept, escalate to legal, or request re-negotiation
5. If escalating: includes AI summary + issue list so legal can work efficiently

## What to Look For in Sales Contract AI

- Handles your document types (MSAs, order forms, SOWs)
- Identifies your specific risk categories
- Fast turnaround (minutes, not hours)
- Easy to use without legal training

## Expected Impact

Sales teams using AI contract pre-screening typically see:
- 30-50% faster contract cycles
- 40-60% reduction in legal queue volume (only real issues escalate)
- Better deal velocity and fewer last-minute surprises

The goal isn't to replace legal — it's to make the legal review that happens more targeted and efficient.
