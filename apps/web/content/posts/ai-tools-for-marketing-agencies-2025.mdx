## The Biggest Time Drains in Agency Work

## Building an AI-First Agency Workflow

The agencies winning in 2025 aren't just using AI tools ad-hoc — they're building systematic workflows.

**A typical AI-augmented client workflow:**

1. **Onboarding** → AI summarizes client's existing content, contracts, and brand guidelines
2. **Strategy calls** → Auto-transcribed and summarized with action items
3. **Research** → AI-assisted industry and competitive analysis
4. **Content creation** → AI-assisted drafting with human editing and strategy
5. **Reporting** → AI extracts insights from data, humans add narrative
6. **Invoicing** → AI processes vendor invoices and client billing automatically

## What Agencies Are Getting Wrong

Most agencies fail at AI adoption because they:

**1. Start too big.** Trying to automate everything at once leads to chaos. Start with one high-value, repetitive task.

**2. Skip the workflow design.** AI tools work best when they fit into a defined process, not as one-off experiments.

**3. Underestimate training needs.** Your team needs to know how to prompt, review, and iterate on AI outputs.

**4. Ignore quality control.** AI output always needs human review. Build review steps into your workflows.

## Practical ROI Calculation

Here's how to calculate AI ROI for your agency:

1. **Identify a repetitive task** (e.g., meeting summarization)
2. **Calculate current time cost** (# of meetings × minutes per summary × hourly rate)

4. **Calculate monthly savings**
