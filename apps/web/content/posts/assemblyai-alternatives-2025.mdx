AssemblyAI is a powerful transcription API. If you're a developer building a product, it's excellent. But if you're a business user — an accountant, consultant, or operations manager — you don't want an API. You want a tool you can use right now.

Here are the five best AssemblyAI alternatives for business users in 2025.

## 1. software-multi-tool

**Best for: Business teams who want AI tools without writing code**

software-multi-tool gives you accurate transcription and speaker separation — plus 7 other AI tools for contracts, invoices, expenses, and feedback. You upload a file, the AI processes it, and you get structured results in seconds.

Unlike AssemblyAI, you don't need to write a single line of code. It's built for business users who want results, not infrastructure.

**Pricing:** Credit-based. No seat fees.

## 2. Otter.ai

**Best for: Meeting notes and transcription**

Otter.ai is the most popular no-code transcription tool for meetings. It connects to Zoom and Google Meet, takes notes automatically, and produces reasonable summaries.

The downside: it only does meetings. If you need to process contracts or invoices, you'll need separate tools.

**Pricing:** Free tier available. Paid plans start around $16/user/month.

## 3. Fireflies.ai

**Best for: Sales teams and CRM integration**

Fireflies.ai is similar to Otter.ai but with stronger CRM integrations. Sales teams like it because it can push meeting notes directly to Salesforce or HubSpot.

Like Otter.ai, it's focused on meetings — not broader document AI.

**Pricing:** Free tier. Paid plans from $18/user/month.

## 4. Rev

**Best for: Accurate transcription with human review options**

Rev offers both AI transcription and human transcription for maximum accuracy. If your recordings have difficult accents, heavy background noise, or technical terminology, Rev's human transcription option is hard to beat.

The tradeoff: human transcription is expensive and slow. AI transcription is faster but comparable to other tools.

**Pricing:** AI transcription from $0.25/min. Human transcription from $1.99/min.

## 5. Descript

**Best for: Podcast and video editors**

Descript turns transcripts into editable video/audio. You edit the transcript and it edits the file. It's a genuinely clever product for podcast producers and video marketers.

It's not the right tool if you need document AI, but it's great if your primary workflow is audio/video editing.

**Pricing:** Free tier. Paid from $24/month.
