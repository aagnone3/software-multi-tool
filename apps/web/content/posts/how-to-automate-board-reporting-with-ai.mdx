# How to Automate Board Reporting with AI

Board reports are one of the most time-intensive documents executives produce — and one of the most consequential. They need to be accurate, concise, and compelling. AI tools are making it possible to produce board-quality materials in a fraction of the traditional time.

## The Board Reporting Challenge

A typical board report cycle involves:

- Gathering data from multiple departments
- Summarizing financial performance
- Reviewing legal and compliance updates
- Preparing slide decks and supporting materials
- Coordinating with board members and counsel

This process often takes 2–3 weeks of part-time effort. AI can compress it significantly.

### Contract and Legal Document Review

Before board meetings, counsel often provides lengthy legal updates. AI can summarize amendments, highlight key obligations, and flag unusual clauses — helping board members prepare without reading every word.

## A Practical Board Reporting Workflow

**Week 3 of the quarter:**
1. Upload financial reports to Document Analyzer → save executive summary
2. Summarize operational updates from department heads via meeting recordings

**Week 4 of the quarter:**
1. Use AI to draft the executive summary section of the board deck
2. Upload legal memos for AI-generated summaries
3. Run AI meeting summarizer on the pre-board call → generate minutes draft

**Day of the board meeting:**
1. Record the board meeting
2. Upload to meeting summarizer immediately after
3. Distribute AI-generated draft minutes within 24 hours

Companies using AI for board reporting report:
- **60% reduction** in preparation time
- **Faster turnaround** on meeting minutes (hours instead of days)
- **More consistent** documentation quality
