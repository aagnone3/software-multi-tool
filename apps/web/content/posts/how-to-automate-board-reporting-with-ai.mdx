Board reporting is one of the most time-consuming activities in any organization. Pulling data, writing narratives, formatting slides — it can take a team days to prepare materials that get reviewed in hours.

AI tools are changing that equation dramatically.

## What Takes So Long About Board Reporting?

The bottlenecks in board reporting usually fall into a few categories:

1. **Data aggregation** — pulling numbers from multiple systems
2. **Narrative writing** — turning data into readable commentary
3. **Document review** — summarizing lengthy reports and analyses
4. **Meeting prep** — capturing and distributing board discussion notes

AI tools can compress each of these significantly.

## AI Tools for Each Stage of Board Reporting

### Document Summarization

Board packets often include lengthy analyst reports, industry studies, and financial statements. AI document summarization tools can turn a 50-page report into a 2-page executive brief with key insights and action items.

**How to use it:**
- Upload analyst reports for quarterly board prep
- Summarize competitor financial filings
- Create executive briefings from research reports

### Meeting Summarization for Board and Exec Meetings

Board meetings produce critical decisions and commitments that need to be accurately captured. AI meeting summarizers transcribe and structure the key outputs.

**How to use it:**
- Record board sessions and generate structured minutes
- Capture action items and assign owners automatically
- Create follow-up summaries to distribute post-meeting

### Financial Document Analysis

AI document analysis tools can extract key metrics, flag changes from prior periods, and identify items requiring board attention.

**How to use it:**
- Review management accounts before board meetings
- Extract key ratios and trends from financial packages
- Identify exceptions and variances requiring explanation
