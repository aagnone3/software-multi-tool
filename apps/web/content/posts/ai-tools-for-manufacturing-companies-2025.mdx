# AI Tools for Manufacturing Companies in 2025

Manufacturing operations are paper-intensive by necessity — quality records, safety documentation, supplier contracts, regulatory filings, and production reports all require careful documentation. AI tools are transforming how manufacturers handle this documentation burden.

## The Documentation Challenge in Manufacturing

A typical mid-size manufacturer manages:

- **Quality documentation**: SOPs, inspection records, non-conformance reports, CAPA documentation
- **Safety documentation**: Incident reports, safety meeting minutes, training records, OSHA compliance
- **Supplier management**: Vendor contracts, quality agreements, purchase orders
- **Regulatory compliance**: ISO standards documentation, FDA records (if applicable), environmental reports
- **Production documentation**: Work orders, production reports, equipment maintenance logs

The manual documentation burden consumes thousands of staff hours annually.

## Compliance and Record-Keeping

Manufacturing documentation often has strict record-keeping requirements. When using AI tools:

1. **Review all AI-generated content** before using in official records
2. **Maintain original source documents** alongside AI summaries
3. **Document AI use** if required by your quality management system
4. **Train staff** on appropriate AI tool use in regulated processes
