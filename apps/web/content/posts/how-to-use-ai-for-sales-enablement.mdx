# How to Use AI for Sales Enablement: Speed Up Your Pipeline

Sales productivity comes down to one thing: time spent with qualified prospects. AI sales enablement tools eliminate the administrative work that keeps reps away from actual selling.

## The Sales Time Problem

Research consistently shows sales reps spend less than 30% of their time actually selling. The rest goes to:
- CRM data entry and updates
- Proposal and document preparation
- Internal reporting
- Research and prospecting
- Following up on administrative tasks

AI can help reclaim most of that time.

## Pre-Call Research and Preparation

### Document Intelligence for Prospect Research
Before any significant meeting, sales reps gather company information, review past interactions, and prepare talking points. AI document summarization can compress hours of research reading into 5-minute briefings.

Key sources to summarize:
- Annual reports and investor materials
- Industry news and press releases
- LinkedIn profiles and activity
- Prior meeting notes and call transcripts

### Contract and Proposal Templates
Standard sales contracts contain predictable sections. AI document analysis can extract key terms from competitor contracts (that prospects share for comparison), flag non-standard clauses, and summarize lengthy agreements.

## During and After Sales Calls

### Call Transcription with Speaker Separation
Every sales call contains valuable information. Win/loss patterns, objection themes, competitor mentions, decision criteria — it's all there if you can analyze it at scale.

AI call transcription with speaker separation gives you:
- Searchable archives of every conversation
- Speaker-specific sentiment analysis
- Objection and topic frequency tracking
- Talk-time ratios (are reps listening or lecturing?)

### Automated Call Summaries
After a call, reps often spend 20-30 minutes writing up notes and updating CRM records. AI meeting summarizers capture that automatically, generating structured summaries with action items.

## Proposal and Document Generation

### Expense and Pricing Intelligence
For complex deals involving travel, consulting hours, or services pricing, AI expense categorization can quickly extract costs from vendor quotes to inform proposal pricing.

### Feedback Analysis for Win/Loss Reviews
Customer feedback from won and lost deals is rarely analyzed systematically. AI sentiment analysis applied to deal feedback surfaces patterns: which objections appear most in losses, which differentiators resonate in wins.

## Implementation Approach

**Week 1-2:** Deploy call transcription and summarization
**Week 3-4:** Set up post-call AI summaries and CRM sync
**Month 2:** Add document analysis for research and contracts
**Month 3:** Implement feedback analysis for win/loss reviews

Measure time-per-deal-stage before and after. Expect to see the biggest gains in research preparation and post-call documentation.
