Accruals are one of the first places close quality drifts.

The entry gets posted, but support is incomplete, stale, or inconsistent with policy. Reviewers then spend close week chasing files and clarifications instead of closing cleanly.

AI can turn accrual support validation into a repeatable control, not a last-minute scramble.

## Where accrual support usually breaks

Common failure points:

- **Missing source documentation for material accruals**
- **Outdated support reused from prior periods**
- **Narratives that don't tie to underlying calculations**
- **Policy mismatches (timing, thresholds, account ownership)**
- **Inconsistent support quality across entities**

Manual checks catch some of this, but not reliably at scale.

## What AI can automate in accrual support validation

### 1) Check support completeness by policy

AI can scan attached files and flag whether required elements exist (calculation, source docs, assumptions, reviewer notes).

### 2) Compare support content to entry metadata

AI can validate that support period, entity, account, and amount align with the posted journal entry.

### 3) Detect stale or reused support artifacts

AI can identify duplicate files, repeated narratives, or prior-period references that suggest copy-forward without refresh.

### 4) Flag weak justifications and unclear assumptions

AI can surface vague language and missing rationale so preparers strengthen support before reviewer escalation.

### 5) Produce reviewer-ready exception packets

For each exception, AI can compile:

- entry details
- missing/weak support elements
- policy rule triggered
- potential risk/materiality context
- recommended remediation step

## Practical rollout plan

1. Define required support fields by accrual type.
2. Encode policy checks (thresholds, required approvals, timing rules).
3. Standardize accrual package naming and metadata tags.
4. Run AI validation daily during close and route exceptions by owner.
5. Track recurring exception themes and update templates.

## Metrics to track

Measure impact over 2-3 close cycles:

- accrual packages passing validation on first submission
- exception rate by entity/account/accrual type
- average time to remediate flagged support gaps
- post-close adjustments tied to unsupported accruals
- audit comments related to accrual documentation quality

## Final takeaway

Accrual quality is mostly a support quality problem.

AI helps finance teams enforce support standards consistently, reduce close rework, and improve audit confidence without adding manual review burden.
