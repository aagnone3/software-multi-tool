Real estate is a paper-heavy business. Contracts, disclosures, inspection reports, market analyses — the document load is relentless. AI tools are changing that.

## The Document Problem in Real Estate

A typical real estate transaction involves 50–100 pages of documents. Reading, comparing, and explaining these to clients is time-consuming. AI document analysis can cut review time by 60–70%.

## Top AI Use Cases for Real Estate Agents

### 1. Contract Analysis
Upload a purchase agreement, addendum, or contingency document and get a plain-English summary of the key terms, deadlines, and obligations.

**Why it matters:** Catch issues before they become problems. Know exactly what your client is signing.

### 2. Market News Monitoring
Stay on top of interest rate changes, local market trends, and economic news without spending hours reading articles every morning.

**Time saved:** 45–60 minutes per day for agents who track multiple markets.

### 3. Meeting Summarization
After client calls, property tours, and team meetings, get AI-generated summaries with action items instead of scrambling to take notes.

### 4. Document Summarization
HOA documents, inspection reports, title commitments — summarize any document in seconds and pull out what actually matters for your clients.

## The Real ROI

One agent who started using AI document tools reported reviewing twice as many listings per week with the same effort. Another saved 3 hours per week just on contract review.

The math is simple: if you bill $500/hour in value and AI saves you 5 hours per week, that's $2,500 per week in recovered capacity.

## Getting Started

The best approach is to start with one use case. Contract analysis tends to have the highest immediate impact — you'll see results on your first uploaded document.

Try processing a recent purchase agreement. You'll immediately see the terms, contingencies, and key dates highlighted without reading every line yourself.
