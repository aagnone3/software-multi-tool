Contracts are full of critical information — but finding it manually is slow and error-prone. AI contract analysis tools can extract payment terms, deadlines, obligations, and risk clauses in minutes instead of hours.

## What Information Can AI Extract from Contracts?

Modern AI document analysis tools can identify and extract:

- **Payment terms** — net 30, net 60, late fees, payment methods
- **Renewal and termination clauses** — auto-renewal dates, notice periods, termination rights
- **Liability limits** — caps, indemnification, warranty exclusions
- **SLA commitments** — uptime guarantees, response times, remedies
- **Key dates** — effective dates, milestones, deadlines
- **Party obligations** — what each side must deliver

## Step-by-Step: Extracting Contract Information with AI

### Step 1: Upload Your Contract

Most AI contract analysis tools accept PDF or Word documents. For best results, use the original digital document rather than a scanned image.

### Step 2: Run the Analysis

The AI will process the document and identify key clauses, obligations, and dates. This typically takes under a minute for most contracts.

### Step 3: Review the Extracted Information

You'll receive a structured summary with the key terms highlighted. Review it against the original document to confirm accuracy.

### Step 4: Export or Act

Export the summary to your contract management system, share it with stakeholders, or use it as the basis for a redline.

## What AI Contract Analysis Can't Do

AI is powerful for extraction and summarization, but it's not a substitute for legal counsel on complex negotiations. Use AI to:

- Speed up first-pass review
- Prepare for negotiations
- Monitor contracts for important dates

Always have legal counsel review significant contracts before signing.
