# How to Reduce Time Spent on Expense Reports

Expense reports are one of those administrative tasks everyone hates but no one can avoid. Receipts to collect, categories to assign, amounts to verify, approvals to chase—it adds up to hours of tedious work every month.

AI tools are changing this workflow dramatically.

## The Hidden Cost of Manual Expense Processing

Consider the typical expense report cycle:

1. **Collect receipts** throughout the month (and inevitably lose some)
2. **Enter data manually** into spreadsheets or expense software
3. **Categorize each transaction** for accounting purposes
4. **Submit for approval** and wait
5. **Answer questions** about unclear entries
6. **Resubmit** if corrections are needed

For a team of 20 employees each spending 3 hours/month: that's 60 person-hours monthly on a process that adds zero business value.

## Where AI Makes the Biggest Difference

### Automatic Categorization

This is the biggest time sink in manual expense processing. AI categorization tools:

- Analyze merchant names and transaction descriptions
- Match them to your expense categories (travel, meals, software, office supplies, etc.)
- Assign categories with high accuracy for common transaction types
- Flag ambiguous transactions for human review

**Software Multi-Tool's expense categorizer** can process an entire month's transaction export in seconds—categorizing each line item automatically.

### Receipt Data Extraction

AI can extract key fields from receipt images:
- Date and time
- Merchant name and category
- Amount and currency
- Payment method

This eliminates manual data entry from paper or PDF receipts.

### Anomaly Detection

AI tools can flag:
- Duplicate submissions
- Amounts outside normal ranges for category
- Missing documentation patterns
- Policy violations (personal expenses mixed with business)

### Month-End Reconciliation

AI can match bank transactions to submitted expenses, flagging gaps and discrepancies automatically.
