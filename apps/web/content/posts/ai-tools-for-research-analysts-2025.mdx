Research analysis involves processing enormous volumes of information — financial reports, industry studies, news, regulatory filings, academic papers — and distilling it into actionable insights. The information-gathering and initial synthesis stages are ripe for AI automation.

## Where AI Adds the Most Value for Research

**Document Summarization**
Long-form documents — annual reports, prospectuses, technical papers — can be summarized quickly. Instead of reading 200 pages, you get a structured summary of the key points, which you then verify and interrogate more deeply.

**Data Extraction**
Structured data buried in PDFs (financial tables, survey results, regulatory filings) can be extracted automatically rather than manually transcribed, reducing errors and saving hours.

**News and Event Monitoring**
AI can monitor and summarize news across a coverage universe — whether that's a set of companies, industries, or geographies — and produce a daily digest of relevant developments.

**First-Draft Generation**
Based on extracted data and summarized sources, AI can generate first drafts of research notes, market commentary, or briefing documents that analysts then refine.

## Specific Workflows That Work

**Equity Research**
- Summarize earnings transcripts for 10-15 companies in a sector during reporting season
- Extract financial metrics from 10-Ks and 10-Qs into a comparable table
- Summarize sell-side research from multiple brokers into a consensus view

**Market Research**
- Process survey reports and extract key findings
- Summarize consumer trend articles into a thematic brief
- Extract pricing data and market share statistics from industry reports

**Policy and Regulatory Research**
- Summarize proposed regulations and extract compliance requirements
- Monitor public comment periods and summarize stakeholder positions
- Extract key dates and obligations from regulatory guidance

## Limitations to Understand

AI tools are not good at:
- Evaluating source credibility
- Making qualitative judgments about management quality or market dynamics
- Generating novel insights not present in the source material

They're excellent at: reducing the time to get from raw information to organized, structured content that an analyst can then apply judgment to.

## Getting Started

The most straightforward entry point for research analysts is document summarization. Start with a specific, recurring document type in your workflow — earnings transcripts, for example — and run them through an AI summarizer consistently for a few weeks.

Once you calibrate what the AI does well and where you need to add judgment, you can expand to other document types.
