Consulting is a high-leverage business: your time is your inventory, and too much of it gets spent on low-value prep work.

Reviewing client contracts. Writing meeting summaries. Categorizing expense reports. Pulling data from invoices.

These tasks are necessary — but they don't require your expertise. They require your time. AI can change that equation.

Here are the tools worth using in 2025.

## 1. Meeting Summarization

**Best tool:** Any AI meeting notetaker (Fathom, Otter.ai, Fireflies)

Client calls generate deliverables — action items, follow-ups, decisions. Capturing those manually while also running the conversation is a split-attention tax.

AI meeting notetakers transcribe and summarize in real time. You get clean notes + action items without lifting a finger.

**Better option for consultants:** An AI tool that also processes the *output* of those meetings — drafting follow-up emails, extracting commitments, or flagging items for client reports.

## 2. Contract Review

**Best tool:** [AppName Contract Analyzer](/tools/contract-analyzer)

Engagement letters, MSAs, SOWs — consultants sign and review a lot of contracts. Most of the review is checklist-style: payment terms, IP ownership, confidentiality, termination clauses.

AI contract analysis runs that checklist in seconds. You get a structured summary of every key clause without reading all 30 pages manually.

What to look for:
- Extracts key terms (payment schedule, liability caps, IP ownership)
- Flags non-standard language
- Outputs structured JSON or human-readable summary
- Works on PDFs without copy-paste
