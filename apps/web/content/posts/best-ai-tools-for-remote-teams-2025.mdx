# Best AI Tools for Remote Teams in 2025

Remote work is here to stay. Here are the AI tools that make distributed teams more effective.

## The Remote Work Challenge

Remote teams face unique challenges:
- Meeting notes scattered across tools
- Documents lost in shared drives
- Asynchronous communication gaps
- Onboarding new team members remotely

## What to Look For

When choosing AI tools for your remote team:
