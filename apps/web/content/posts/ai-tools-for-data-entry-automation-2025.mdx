# How AI Is Eliminating Manual Data Entry

Manual data entry costs businesses billions in lost productivity and errors every year. AI is finally solving this.

## The Cost of Manual Data Entry

- Average error rate of 1-4% for manual entry
- Hours lost per week per employee
- Downstream costs from incorrect data
- Employee frustration and turnover

## AI Approaches to Data Entry Automation

### OCR + AI Extraction
Optical character recognition combined with AI can extract structured data from PDFs, images, and scanned documents with high accuracy.

### Invoice Processing
AI can extract vendor name, line items, totals, and payment terms from any invoice format automatically.

### Form Processing
Structured forms (applications, surveys, intake forms) can be processed automatically without manual keying.

### Receipt Processing
Expense receipts can be photographed and automatically categorized and entered into expense systems.

## ROI of AI Data Entry

For a team processing 100 documents per week:
- Manual: 5 hours at 5/hour = 25/week
- AI-assisted: 30 minutes review = 2.50/week
- Annual savings: ,850 per team member
