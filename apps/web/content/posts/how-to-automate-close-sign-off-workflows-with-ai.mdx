Sign-off is where many close processes stall.

Work gets completed, but approvals sit in inboxes, ownership is unclear, and the close lead has to manually chase every dependency.

AI helps finance teams run sign-off like a system, not a scramble.

## Why close sign-off gets delayed

Common bottlenecks include:

- **Approval requests are inconsistent** across teams and entities
- **Evidence is scattered** across spreadsheets, exports, and chat
- **Escalations happen too late** because aging is tracked manually
- **Final approvers lack context** to approve quickly and confidently
- **Audit trail quality is uneven** across close cycles

When sign-off is inconsistent, close timelines slip and review quality drops.

## What AI can automate in sign-off workflows

### 1) Readiness checks before approval routing

AI can verify each sign-off package has required evidence before routing:

- reconciliations attached
- variance commentary present
- unresolved exceptions listed
- owner attestation included

### 2) Smart approval routing

AI can route approvals based on entity, materiality, and risk profile instead of one static workflow.

### 3) Context-rich approval summaries

For each approver, AI can generate a short summary of:

- what changed since prior period
- key risks and open items
- why this package is ready (or not)

### 4) Escalation and SLA monitoring

AI can trigger escalation by aging threshold and risk, not just calendar reminders.

## Practical rollout plan

1. Define a standard sign-off checklist by account/process type.
2. Capture required evidence fields in one structured template.
3. Add AI validation to block incomplete submissions before routing.
4. Implement risk-based approval queues and SLA alerts.
5. Track late approvals and root causes each close cycle.

## Metrics to track

Over the next 2-3 close cycles, measure:

- percent of sign-off packages submitted complete on first pass
- average approval cycle time by process
- overdue approvals by risk level
- number of escalation events per cycle
- audit rework requests tied to missing evidence

## Final takeaway

Close sign-off should be predictable, fast, and auditable.

AI helps finance teams enforce sign-off quality at scale so approvals move faster without sacrificing control.
