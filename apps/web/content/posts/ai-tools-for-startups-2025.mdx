When you're a startup, every hour counts. You're competing against companies with 10x your headcount, and the only way to win is to be dramatically more efficient.

## The Startup AI Stack

### 1. Meeting Summarizer — Stop Wasting Time on Recap Emails

Every startup has too many meetings. The AI meeting summarizer automatically generates structured summaries with action items so you never have to write recap emails again.

Upload your meeting transcript or recording and get:
- Executive summary
- Key decisions made
- Action items with owners
- Follow-up questions

**Time saved**: 20–40 minutes per meeting

### 2. Contract Analyzer — Protect Yourself Without a Lawyer on Speed Dial

Early-stage startups sign vendor contracts, SaaS agreements, and NDAs constantly. Paying lawyers $500/hour to read every contract isn't realistic.

The contract analyzer flags:
- Problematic clauses (auto-renewal, data ownership, IP assignment)
- Payment terms and late fees
- Termination conditions
- Liability limitations

**Time saved**: 2–4 hours per contract review

### 3. Feedback Analyzer — Turn Customer Feedback into Product Decisions

If you're doing customer discovery or collecting NPS surveys, you need to analyze patterns — not read every response manually.

The feedback analyzer processes hundreds of responses and surfaces:
- Common themes and sentiment
- Feature requests ranked by frequency
- Pain points by customer segment
- Verbatim quotes for each theme

**Time saved**: 4–8 hours per feedback cycle

### 4. Expense Categorizer — Month-End Close Without the Pain

Founders and ops leads spend hours every month categorizing expenses for their accountant. The expense categorizer handles this automatically.

Upload your bank export CSV and get:
- Categorized transactions (SaaS, travel, payroll, marketing, etc.)
- Anomaly flags for duplicate charges
- Summary by category for easy accounting handoff

**Time saved**: 3–5 hours per month
