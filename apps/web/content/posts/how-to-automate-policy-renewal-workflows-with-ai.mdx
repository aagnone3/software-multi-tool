# How to Automate Policy Renewal Workflows with AI

Policy renewal season creates repetitive work: review documents, compare changes, identify risk shifts, and notify clients.

AI can automate large parts of this process while keeping brokers in control of the final recommendation.

## Where Renewal Workflows Usually Break

Most teams struggle with:

- tracking renewal dates across multiple systems
- manually comparing prior vs current policy terms
- summarizing changes for client-facing communication
- prioritizing renewals that need immediate attention

These are ideal automation candidates.

## A Simple AI Renewal Workflow

### 1) Ingest renewal documents

Collect policy files, endorsements, and prior-year summaries into one pipeline.

### 2) Extract key fields

Use AI to capture dates, limits, deductibles, exclusions, and premium changes.

### 3) Compare with prior policy

Generate a structured diff of what changed and why it matters.

### 4) Prioritize by risk and urgency

Score renewals by change severity, claim history, and renewal window.

### 5) Draft client communication

Prepare a concise client summary that explains changes and recommended actions.

## Tools You Can Use

- [Software Multi-Tool](/) for document extraction, summarization, and comparison workflows
- your CRM/AMS for renewal status tracking
- email automation for staged reminder campaigns

## Implementation Tips

- Start with one line of business before scaling
- Keep a human approval step before client delivery
- Track turnaround time and retention impact monthly
- Build templates for recurring communication types

## Final Takeaway

AI renewal automation is most effective when it removes repetitive analysis but keeps brokers in the decision loop.

If your team still builds renewal briefs manually, this is one of the fastest workflows to modernize.
