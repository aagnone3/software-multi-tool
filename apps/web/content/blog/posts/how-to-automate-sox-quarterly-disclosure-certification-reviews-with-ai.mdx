Quarterly disclosure certification reviews are one of the most labor-intensive parts of SOX compliance. CFOs and controllers must certify that financial statements fairly present the company's financial condition — and that process involves reviewing hundreds of supporting documents under tight deadlines.

<MidPostCTA />

## The Manual Review Bottleneck

Traditional quarterly disclosure certification reviews involve:

- Collecting certifications from business unit controllers
- Reviewing supporting disclosure checklists
- Cross-referencing prior quarter certifications for consistency
- Identifying open items and escalating for resolution
- Assembling the final CEO/CFO certification package

When done manually, this process takes 2–4 weeks per quarter and is vulnerable to human error.

## Where AI Helps

AI document analysis tools can dramatically accelerate this workflow:

**Document Ingestion**: Upload prior certifications, current period disclosures, and supporting schedules. AI extracts key assertions, open items, and sign-off status.

**Consistency Checks**: AI compares current period assertions against prior quarters, flagging any language changes that require explanation.

**Open Item Triage**: AI identifies unresolved items from prior periods and checks whether they appear in the current package.

**Package Assembly**: AI drafts the certification package narrative based on extracted data, ready for controller review.

## Implementation Approach

1. Establish a standard template for business unit sub-certifications
2. Collect digital submissions in a central repository
3. Run AI analysis to extract key data points from each submission
4. Generate a consolidated summary highlighting anomalies
5. Route flagged items to responsible parties for resolution
6. Finalize CEO/CFO certification package

## Practical Benefits

Organizations using AI-assisted disclosure certification reviews report:

- 40–60% reduction in review cycle time
- Fewer late submissions due to proactive tracking
- Improved completeness through automated checklist validation
- Better audit trail for external auditors

## Getting Started

Start small: pilot the AI review process on one business unit's sub-certification for a single quarter. Use the results to build confidence and refine the workflow before rolling out company-wide.

The goal isn't to replace human judgment — it's to give your team better information faster so they can make sound certification decisions with confidence.
