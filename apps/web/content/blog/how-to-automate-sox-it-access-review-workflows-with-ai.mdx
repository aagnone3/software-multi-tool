# How to Automate SOX IT Access Review Workflows with AI

Periodic user access reviews are a cornerstone of SOX IT General Controls (ITGCs). Auditors expect evidence that privileged access is granted on a least-privilege basis, that terminated employees are removed promptly, and that access certifications are completed by business owners — not just IT.

Manually pulling reports from identity systems, reconciling them against HR data, chasing managers for sign-off, and packaging evidence for auditors is slow, inconsistent, and scales poorly as your company grows.

AI can automate the repetitive steps and focus your team on judgment-intensive decisions.

## What Makes SOX Access Reviews Hard

1. **Data from multiple systems** — Active Directory, Salesforce, ERP, cloud IAM, and HR systems rarely sync automatically
2. **Volume** — Even a mid-size public company may have 10,000+ user-application combinations to review quarterly
3. **Manager fatigue** — Certifications that land in inboxes without context get rubber-stamped or ignored
4. **Auditor expectations** — Evidence must show who reviewed, when, and what action was taken — not just a checkbox

## How AI Automates Each Step

### 1. Data Aggregation and Reconciliation

AI can ingest user lists from Active Directory, HR termination feeds, and application role reports, then automatically:

- Flag terminated employees with active access
- Identify accounts that haven't logged in for 90+ days
- Highlight privilege creep (users who accumulated roles without removal)
- Cross-reference access against job title and department

Upload your reports as CSV or connect via API. AI extracts the signal before reviewers ever see a spreadsheet.

### 2. Risk Scoring Access Exceptions

Not all access exceptions carry the same risk. AI can score each exception based on:

- Application sensitivity (financial systems > productivity tools)
- User role and department alignment
- Time since last activity
- Whether the user is in a sensitive role (admin, finance, C-suite)

High-risk exceptions get escalated immediately. Low-risk dormant accounts can be auto-queued for removal without manager review.

### 3. Manager Certification Workflow

Instead of sending managers a raw spreadsheet, AI can:

- Generate a plain-English summary for each user under review ("Jane Doe has admin access to your ERP — last login 6 months ago. Certify or remove?")
- Route certifications to the correct manager based on current HR data
- Send automated reminders with escalation to department heads after 48 hours of no response
- Log every action with timestamp for audit evidence

### 4. Exception Resolution Tracking

For access that needs removal or remediation, AI can:

- Create tickets in your ITSM (ServiceNow, Jira) automatically
- Track remediation status against review deadlines
- Flag overdue items with severity escalation
- Generate a final exception report showing what was remediated and when

### 5. Audit Evidence Packaging

Once the review is complete, AI assembles:

- A summary report showing % certified, % removed, % escalated
- Supporting detail for any exceptions that required manual judgment
- Timestamps and reviewer IDs for all certifications
- A cover memo suitable for external auditors

This replaces what used to be a multi-day packaging exercise with a same-day export.

## Sample Prompt for AI-Assisted Access Review

> "Here is our Q1 user access extract for Salesforce [attached CSV]. Cross-reference against the attached terminated employee list. Flag any terminated users with active accounts, any accounts inactive >90 days, and any accounts where the application role doesn't match the user's current department. Group findings by risk level and generate a manager certification task list."

## Integration Points

| System | What AI Reads | What AI Writes |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| Active Directory | User list, last login, groups | Removal queue |
| HRIS (Workday, BambooHR) | Terminations, role changes | N/A |
| ERP (NetSuite, SAP) | Application roles | Exception report |
| ITSM (Jira, ServiceNow) | N/A | Remediation tickets |
| Email/Slack | N/A | Manager certification requests |

## Metrics to Track

- **Review completion rate** — % of access certifications completed before deadline
- **Remediation SLA** — average days to remove flagged access after identification
- **Exception rate** — % of access items requiring action (a leading indicator of access hygiene)
- **Auditor query rate** — how often auditors request additional evidence post-review (tracks whether your packaging is sufficient)

## Getting Started

You don't need a full IAM overhaul to benefit from AI-assisted access reviews. Start with:

1. Pull your most sensitive application's user access report (start with your ERP or financial reporting system)
2. Run it through an AI tool with your HR termination list
3. Generate your first risk-scored exception list
4. Use that output to pilot the manager certification workflow

Most teams see 60–70% reduction in manual effort by the second review cycle.

---

SOX IT access reviews are one of the most document-intensive quarterly tasks in a public company's compliance calendar. AI won't replace the human judgment involved in complex exceptions — but it will eliminate the spreadsheet-wrangling that consumes most of the time.
